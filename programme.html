Event Programme

3 Days of Adventure, Culture and Entertainment

Programme Schedule

Time Activity Description
Friday: Arrival & Kick-off
12:00 PM Arrival & Setup Campers arrive at campsite (2,260m) and set up Tent City.
4:00 PM Opening Entertainment Live DJs and performers begin the festival vibe.
8:00 PM Evening Social Campfires, chill zones and networking under the stars.
Saturday: The Main Braai Experience
10:00 AM The Big Braai Massive open-air grilling event with vendors and guests.
11:30 AM Abseiling World’s longest single-drop abseil (192m).
1:00 PM Pony Trekking Explore highlands on horseback.
2:30 PM Helicopter Rides Scenic aerial views of Maletsunyane Falls.
6:00 PM Live Music Festival Top Southern African artists perform live.
8:30 PM Cultural Showcase Mokhibo & Mohobelo dances and exhibitions.
Sunday: Adventure & Departure
8:00 AM Morning Exploration Group gorge hiking and quad biking in Semonkong.
10:00 AM Scenic Sightseeing Guided tours of waterfalls and heritage sites.
11:30 AM Farewell Vibes Final live music and food stalls before departure.
