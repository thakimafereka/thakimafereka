Introducton to Semonkong


Semonkong, meaning "Place of Smoke" in Sesotho, is a place found and breed in the highland town situated in the Maseru Lesotho.It has an altitude of approximately 2,275 meters (7,464 feet) within the Maluti Mountains, it is famously home to the Maletsunyane Falls, one of the highest single-drop waterfalls in Africa. It was stablished in the early 1880s, Semonkong originally served as a sanctuary for Basotho people displaced by the Basuto Gun War. Basotho use horses as means of transport in the highlands. Its town is often known as the "horse capital" of Lesotho. Horses remain a primary mode of transport for locals navigating the steep mountain passes, and you will frequently see residents clad in traditional Basotho blankets riding through town. It is very cold that temperatures can drop below zero even in summer at the base of the gorge, and snow is common during winter months
